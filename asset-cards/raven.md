The Raven doesn't have HP. If it would take damage, it instead disappears in a plume of black feathers (for now). Wherever you go, the Raven is always there before you.

- When you <ins>Begin a Session</ins> if you choose to envision a brief vignette from the perspective of the Raven, instead of taking +1 momentum, if:
    - It spotlights a known NPC, each player takes +1 spirit.
    - It reveals something from a PC's past, each player takes +2 momentum.
    - It reveals a new danger or obstacle, each player takes +1 on their next move.

- When you <ins>Set a Course</ins> by following the Raven while inside a wizard's Demesne, add +2.

- When you take this upgrade, the Raven brings you an Item. Take it as an asset for free. When you <ins>Continue a Legacy</ins>, envision how the Raven finds the new character, and gives them that item as well.
