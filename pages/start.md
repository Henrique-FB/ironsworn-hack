# Arcana


## Premise

Years ago, a group of people fed up with the horrors brought on by the cursed Arcana - a magical energy no one knows the source of - started hunting down and murdering the wizards that made use of this strange power.

This group of people grew larger and larger, and became known as members of The Covenant.

Through the course of play you will create (and lose) multiple characters, all members of this Covenant. They will be completely different people, with different goals, aspirations, relationships, but all hunters with a deep hatred of wizards.

## Default Assumptions

When you are creating the world your game will take place in, there are some default assumptions to keep in mind:

- **It's a gritty setting.** People are oppressed by both other people and the harsh world around them.
- **It's a bleak setting.** Moments of coziness and happyness are few and far between.
- **It's a grey setting.** Most people are fighting for their survival and their own goals. 
- **It's _not_ a lonely setting.** People form communities to survive. Families; Kingdoms; Guilds; Churches. Everyone knows someone else that can do the job they can't.
- **It's _not_ a highly technological setting.** Technology may exist in the form of steam powdered metal machinery, trains, funcional prosthetics, but it is either scarse, expensive, or extremely dangerous. 
- **It's, at times, a hopeful setting.** Even through the harshness of the world, the small moments of warmth fill the people with hope.

### The Covenant's members
- The Covenant is comprised of highly skilled fighters, trained from a very young age. Once every year a group of members of the Covenant will go through each nearby town and choose a kid, usually a teenager, to bring into the Covenant.
- The Covenant acepts everyone willing to help it. Farmers, merchants, nobleman, as long as you are willing to throw your life away in the service of killing wizards, you are a sibling to the Covenant's members.
- To join the Covenant you must be born, or married, into it. As a tradition that has been carried through generations, the art of hunting wizards and dealing with the Arcana is a highly guarded secret. 

How did you get into the Covenant? *Why* did you get into the Covenant?

### Arcana

- Arcana is the essence of magic. In small amounts it can be seen as a harmless kind of vapour. But when highly concentrated it condenses and warps reality around it in bizarre ways.

- Arcana is the energy that resides in everything. Every aspect of the world has some Arcana in it, but when it is tamed - or, when someone attempts to tame it - it grows erratic, chaotic, and changes everything around it.

- Knowing anything about Arcana and how it functions is viewed as wizardry. General knowledge of it is scarce, and barely anyone knows how if truly functions.

### Wizards

- Wizards are filthy, vile human beings that care for nothing but their own goals.
